For the first time ever, the social event of the year is open to all. The only policy is nothing new.

Every year, 10 million tonnes of clothing end up in landfill in North America. As drivers of fashion innovation, we are taking the step forward. This MET Gala, there will be no new garments. All guests are challenged to give new life to textiles and other materials that would normally be deemed as waste. As always, 'fashion's biggest night out' will be raising money for The Metropolitan Museum of Art Costume Institute.

Join us for an unforgettable night of rebirth as the industry enters into a new chapter.
